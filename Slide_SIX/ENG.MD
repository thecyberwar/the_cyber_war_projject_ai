## English Description

In this slide, we present the **major problems in modern cybersecurity systems.**

The first issue is the **rapid growth of cyber attacks**.  
In today’s digital world, cyber attacks are increasing rapidly, putting sensitive data and systems at risk.

The second problem is the **lack of real-time threat intelligence**.  
Often, organizations do not receive security threat information quickly enough to respond effectively.

The third challenge is the **difficulty in monitoring the deep web and dark web**.  
These environments sometimes contain information about cybercrime and data leaks, but tracking them is very difficult.

Another major issue is that **many cybersecurity tools are too complex for beginners**.  
New learners often find it difficult to understand and use these tools effectively.

There is also a **lack of AI-driven cyber defense systems** that can automatically analyze and detect threats.

Finally, there are **limited automated penetration testing solutions**, which means many security tests still require manual effort.

To address these challenges, we proposed an **AI-based cybersecurity system**.
